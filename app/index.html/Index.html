Report New Cases

For ASHA workers and community volunteers

Hygiene & Prevention

Clean Water: Always drink from a safe, clean water source. Boil water if necessary.

Hand Washing: Wash hands thoroughly with soap and water before eating and after using the toilet.

Proper Sanitation: Use and maintain clean toilets. Dispose of waste properly to avoid contamination.
